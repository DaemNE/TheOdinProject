Recipe for authentic Japanese Sushi!

Description

Traditional, full-flavored sushi is rolled in just minutes with fresh nori, salmon, avocado and sweet rice.

Perfect to eat with your girlfriend

Ingredients

  • 500gr rice
  • Enough water
  • 50ml Rice vinegar
  • 1 Cucumber
  • 200gr Fresh Salmon
  • 1 avocado

Steps

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Let cool off the rice and add the rice vinegar
  3. Spread the rice on some nori
  4. Add ingredients as you
  5. Roll your sushi
  6. Enjoy!
